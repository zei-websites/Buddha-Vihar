Our Story

A restaurant first, a celebration space when it matters.

Buddha Vihar started as a straightforward vegetarian dining room in Hajipur — the kind of place families come back to for an unhurried breakfast or a quick lunch. The name means “a resting place”, and that's still the idea: good food, no rush.

Every now and then a table turns into a small birthday party — a few balloons, a photo wall, a cake cut between courses. Nothing grand, just a proper little celebration in the middle of an ordinary week.
